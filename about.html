About Us

Cozy PG was founded in 2015 with the mission of providing comfortable and affordable accommodation to students and professionals. Over the years, we have helped thousands of individuals find their home away from home. Our PG rooms are equipped with modern amenities, high-speed Wi-Fi, and 24/7 security, ensuring a safe and welcoming environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make your stay comfortable, convenient, and secure. We strive to create a warm and welcoming community where you feel at home. Our team is committed to providing the best possible experience for every resident.
